Who We Are

Built for creatives,
by creatives.

Malayalam Filmmakers was founded by Paul under LowLightKing Productions Pvt Ltd, Kochi — with one idea in mind: that the best work happens when the right people are in the same room.

We are a community-first platform where Malayalam cinema and content creators share knowledge, opportunities, and good energy. No gatekeeping, no egos — just craft.
